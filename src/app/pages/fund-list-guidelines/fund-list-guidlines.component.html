GOVERNMENT FUND LIST GUIDELINES
Do's
  1. Ensure that the information you submit is accurate and up to date when applying for funding.
  2. Read through all eligibility criteria carefully before applying to avoid wasting time on programs you don't qualify for.
  3. Use the platform's search and filter tools to find relevant funding programs that suit your startup's needs and location.
  4. Apply through the official government channels linked on the platform to ensure your application is processed correctly.
  5. Gather all necessary documents and information before beginning your application to avoid delays.
  6. Regularly check for updates on the platform, as funding programs may change or expire over time.
Dont's
  1. Do not apply for programs you are clearly not eligible for.
  2. Do not submit incomplete or inaccurate information when applying for funding.
  3. Do not apply without fully understanding the requirements and obligations of each program.
  4. Do not share your personal or financial information with unverified sources outside of official application portals.
  5. Do not use the platform to promote irrelevant programs or services.
  6. Do not ignore updates to funding programs; always ensure you're using current information.

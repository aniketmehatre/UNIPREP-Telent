COMPANY LIST GUIDELINES
  1. Thorough Research: Take the time to thoroughly research each company listed, understanding their industry, mission, and values.
  2. Tailored Outreach: Personalize your communication with companies, demonstrating your interest and alignment with their goals.
  3. Highlight Relevant Skills: Showcase your skills and experiences that are relevant to the company and the positions they offer.
  4. Professional Communication: Maintain a professional tone and demeanor in all interactions with companies, whether through emails, applications, or interviews.
  5. Utilize Contact Details Appropriately: Use provided contact details responsibly, reaching out only for genuine inquiries or applications related to job opportunities or other relevant matters.
  1. Mass Applications: Avoid sending generic or mass job applications to multiple companies, as this can diminish your credibility and chances of success.
  2. Overcommunication: Refrain from bombarding companies with excessive inquiries or follow-ups, respecting their time and processes.
  3. Misrepresentation: Do not misrepresent your skills, experiences, or intentions when contacting or applying to companies.
  4. Ignoring Job Requirements: Avoid applying to positions for which you do not meet the required qualifications or criteria, as this wastes both your time and the company's.
  5. Unsolicited Marketing: Do not use company contact details for unsolicited marketing or sales purposes, as this may result in negative consequences for your reputation and future interactions.

UNIPREP Refund Policy

Thank you for choosing UNIPREP, hosted by UNIABROAD. We value your trust and are committed to providing a transparent refund policy to ensure a satisfactory user experience.


1. Free Registration -

  • Users who opt for the free registration with a free trial period will not be charged during this period.
  • No cancellation fees will be applied under any circumstances.
  • If users decide not to continue after the trial, no charges will be incurred.

2. Paid Subscription Plans:

  • UNIPREP offers various subscription plans based on validity and the number of countries selected for information access (up to 13 countries).
  • Regardless of the subscription plan chosen, there will be no cancellation fees imposed.
  • UNIPREP does not provide refunds for any subscription plans once they are activated.
  • It's essential to note that when you upgrade your subscription, the previous subscription is automatically discontinued, and the new subscription begins immediately. No refund will be issued for the previous plan

Cancellation Process:

  • Users can cancel their subscription at any time without incurring any charges.
  • To cancel, please contact the UNIPREP team at support@uniprep.ai with your request.

Contact Information:

  • For more information or assistance, feel free to reach out to the UNIPREP team at support@uniprep.ai

Note:

  • UNIPREP reserves the right to modify the refund policy at its discretion.
  • Users are encouraged to review the terms regularly for any updates. Thank you for being a part of UNIPREP! We are here to support your educational journey.

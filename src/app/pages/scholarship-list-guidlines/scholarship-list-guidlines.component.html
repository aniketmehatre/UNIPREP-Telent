SCHOLARSHIP LIST GUIDELINES
  1. Thoroughly Review Eligibility Criteria: Carefully read through the eligibility criteria for each scholarship to ensure you meet the requirements before applying.
  2. Submit Complete and Accurate Applications: Provide all required documents and information accurately and promptly when applying for scholarships.
  3. Tailor Applications: Customize your scholarship applications to highlight your strengths, experiences, and achievements relevant to the scholarship criteria.
  4. Seek Additional Information: Reach out to scholarship providers for clarification or additional information when needed, ensuring you have a clear understanding of the application process.
  5. Apply to Multiple Scholarships: Apply to multiple scholarships to increase your chances of receiving financial aid for studying abroad.
  1. Sell Data: Do not sell or misuse any data obtained from the scholarship lists on UNIPREP or engage in any unethical practices related to data privacy.
  2. Ignore Application Instructions: Follow all application instructions provided by scholarship providers carefully, ensuring you meet all requirements.
  3. Plagiarize or Misrepresent: Do not plagiarize application essays or misrepresent your qualifications, achievements, or financial need when applying for scholarships.
  4. Disregard Scholarship Terms: Pay attention to scholarship terms and conditions, including any obligations or requirements associated with receiving the scholarship.
  5. Apply only if you are eligible: Apply to the scholarships only if you are eligible for them. Do not apply without checking your eligibility.
